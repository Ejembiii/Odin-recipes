Rice

Description

Rice is the seed of a grass species Oryza sativa (Asian rice) or less commonly Oryza glaberrima (African rice).

As a cereal grain, domesticated rice is the most widely consumed staple food for half of the world's human population, especially in Asia and Africa.

Steps

    Steps in preparing rice.
  1. Rinse rice to remove starch.
  2. Soak or perboil rice to improve texture and expansion.
  3. Boil the rice in a pot.
  4. Add all necessary ingredients.
  5. Test the rice to know if it's “ready”.
  6. Save a scoop of rice water.
  7. Now serve hot.
